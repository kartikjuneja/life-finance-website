Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data stored securely? ▼

Yes. Life Finance uses local-first storage, meaning all your financial data stays on your device. We don't send sensitive information to external servers. Your data is encrypted and protected by your device's security features. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest security patches.

Can I recover deleted transactions? ▼

Once deleted, transactions cannot be recovered unless you have a backup. We strongly recommend regularly exporting your data as a backup. Use the Data Export feature to create periodic backups that you can store safely. This protects you against accidental data loss.

How do I backup my data? ▼

Life Finance allows you to export all your financial data at any time. Go to Settings → Data Export and choose your preferred format (CSV or JSON). Keep these backups in a secure location, such as cloud storage or an external drive. Regular backups ensure you never lose important financial records.

Does Life Finance provide financial advice? ▼

No. Life Finance is a personal finance management tool designed to help you track and organize your finances. It does not provide financial, investment, or tax advice. Always consult with a qualified financial advisor or tax professional for investment decisions and tax planning.

Can I sync my data across multiple devices? ▼

Currently, Life Finance stores data locally on your device. For multi-device access, use the Data Export feature to backup your data and manually import it on another device. Cloud sync is on our roadmap as an optional feature with end-to-end encryption.

What file formats can I import? ▼

Life Finance currently supports importing and exporting data in CSV and JSON formats. These formats are compatible with most spreadsheet and data analysis tools, making it easy to work with your financial data in other applications.

Is Life Finance available on iOS and Android? ▼

Life Finance is currently available on Android. iOS support is being developed and will be released soon. You'll be able to download it from the Google Play Store and Apple App Store respectively. Stay tuned for the iOS launch.

What should I do if the app crashes? ▼

If Life Finance crashes, please report it using the bug reporting feature or contact us directly at support@lifefi.com with details about when and how the crash occurred. Your feedback helps us improve the app. Include your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue if possible.

Data Export

1

Open Settings

2

Choose Export Format

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. Delete individual items or perform a complete account reset at any time.

1

Delete Individual Transactions

Swipe or tap the delete icon on any transaction to remove it. Deleted transactions cannot be recovered unless you have a backup.

⚠ Warning: This action is permanent. Consider exporting your data first as a backup.

2

Clear Account Data

To delete all financial data from Life Finance:

  • Go to Settings → Data Management
  • Select "Clear All Data"
  • Confirm the action (you'll need to enter your PIN or authentication)

⚠ Warning: This will permanently delete all transactions, accounts, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

3

Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Life Finance will not automatically delete your data from the device. Manually clear app data through your device settings for complete removal.

  • Android: Settings → Apps → Life Finance → Storage → Clear Cache/Clear Data
  • iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Life Finance → Offload App
4

Request Data Deletion

If you need additional assistance with data deletion or have privacy concerns, contact our support team:

Email: privacy@lifefi.com Subject: Data Deletion Request Include: Your account details and specific deletion requests

Data Retention Policy

We do not retain copies of deleted data on our servers (as we store data locally). Once deleted from your device, it's permanently gone. For complete data deletion, contact our privacy team.
