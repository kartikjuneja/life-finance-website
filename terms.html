1. Service Description

Life Finance is a personal finance management application for Android devices. The app allows you to:

  • Track your financial accounts (checking, savings, investments, credit cards)
  • Monitor assets and liabilities
  • Record and categorize transactions
  • Generate financial reports and visualizations
  • Plan budgets and financial goals
  • Manage recurring transactions and reminders

Life Finance is a personal finance tracking tool only. It does not connect to any financial institutions, execute transactions, or move money on your behalf.
