Privacy Policy

At Life Finance, your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in our Android app.

Last updated: June 20, 2024 | Effective for Life Finance Android App

Overview

Life Finance is designed with your privacy as the foundation. The app operates on a local-first, privacy-focused architecture:

  • Offline First: The app functions completely offline without requiring internet connectivity.
  • Local Storage: All financial data is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Cloud Requirement: We do not require cloud storage or account creation.
  • No Data Sales: We never sell, trade, or share your financial information with third parties.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behavior or financial activities.

Information We Collect

Life Finance minimizes data collection. Since the app operates offline and locally:

Financial Data (Stored Locally)

The following information is entered by you and stored exclusively on your device:

  • Account information (account names, account types, balances)
  • Asset details (real estate, vehicles, investments, valuations)
  • Liability information (loans, mortgages, credit cards, debt)
  • Transaction history (dates, amounts, categories, descriptions)
  • Recurring transaction rules and schedules
  • Institution details (bank names, account numbers, routing information)
  • Category and tag assignments

App Metadata (Minimal)

To function properly, the app may collect:

  • Device identifiers (for app identification only, not for tracking)
  • App version and build information
  • Android OS version

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or calendar data
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Biometric data (unless used for app unlock only)
  • Payment information or bank credentials

Data Storage

Life Finance uses local device storage exclusively:

Local Storage

All financial data is stored in encrypted local storage on your Android device using Android's internal storage mechanisms. This storage is:

  • Protected by your device's security settings
  • Accessible only to the Life Finance app
  • Backed up according to your device's backup settings
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app (if automatic cleanup is enabled)

No Cloud Storage

We do not:

  • Store your financial data on remote servers
  • Automatically sync data to the cloud
  • Use third-party cloud services for data storage
  • Access your stored data remotely

Optional Cloud Backup

In future releases, optional encrypted cloud backup may be available. This feature would be:

  • Completely optional and off by default
  • End-to-end encrypted (encrypted before leaving your device)
  • Never accessible to Life Finance developers
  • Fully under your control with ability to delete anytime

App Permissions

Life Finance requests minimal permissions necessary for core functionality:

Permission Purpose Required
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Import financial data from files (CSV, etc.) Optional
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Export financial data or backup files Optional
INTERNET Future optional cloud backup feature only Optional
CAMERA Future OCR feature to scan receipts Optional
USE_BIOMETRIC Optional biometric authentication for app unlock Optional

Note: We never request permissions we don't need. You can deny optional permissions, and the app will continue to function with core features.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

Life Finance currently does not include third-party analytics or automatic crash reporting services.

What We Track

  • We do not send any analytics data to remote servers
  • We do not track user behavior or app usage
  • We do not use Google Analytics, Crashlytics, or similar services

Future Improvements

If we implement crash reporting in the future, it will be:

  • Completely optional and off by default
  • Limited to crash stacktraces only (no financial data)
  • Clearly disclosed to you
  • Fully under your control

Your Rights

You have complete control over your financial data:

Access

You have immediate access to all your data stored on your device at any time through the Life Finance app.

Data Deletion

You can delete any or all of your data at any time through the app:

  • Delete individual transactions, accounts, or assets
  • Clear all data and reset the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

Data Portability

You can export your data from Life Finance:

  • Export to CSV format for use in other applications
  • Create backups of your complete financial data
  • Transfer your data to other financial apps

Opt-Out

You can opt out of any optional features:

  • Optional crash reporting (when implemented)
  • Optional cloud backup (when implemented)
  • Optional analytics (if ever implemented)

Data Deletion

Your financial data remains under your complete control:

How to Delete Your Data

  • Individual Items: Delete specific transactions, accounts, assets, or rules within the app
  • All Data: Use the "Clear All Data" option in app settings to remove everything
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove Life Finance and all stored data from your device
  • Manual Storage Access: You can manually delete the app's data folder through your device's file system

Data Retention

Since we don't store data on our servers, there is no retention policy from our end. Once you delete data from your device, it is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Life Finance is designed for personal financial management and is not intended for children under 13.

Our Commitment

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • The app contains no marketing or data collection practices targeting minors
  • If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us immediately

Parents and guardians: If your child has access to this app, we recommend enabling device-level parental controls and security features.

Security

We take security seriously and employ multiple layers of protection:

Local Encryption

  • Data is encrypted at rest using Android's encrypted storage mechanisms
  • Optional app-level PIN or biometric authentication for access control
  • Secure data handling practices in all app operations

What We Don't Store Remotely

  • We cannot be hacked for your financial data (it's not on our servers)
  • No third-party access to your financial information
  • No risk from data breaches of cloud services we use

Your Device Security

Since data is stored locally, your device's security is crucial:

  • Keep your Android device updated with the latest security patches
  • Use a strong device lock (PIN, password, or biometric)
  • Avoid using the app on rooted/jailbroken devices
  • Enable Google Play Protect and other device security features

Important Disclaimers

Life Finance is a personal financial tracking and management tool. We do not provide banking services, hold funds, perform financial transactions, or act as a financial institution.
